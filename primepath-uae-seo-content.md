### Red Flags to Watch For

**H2:** Protect Yourself from Recruitment Fraud

International job seekers are frequently targeted by scammers posing as recruitment agents. Protect yourself:

- 🚩 Never pay a recruitment fee to get a job. Legitimate agencies are paid by employers.
- 🚩 Be cautious of offers that arrive without a formal interview or proper employment contract.
- 🚩 Verify the company exists and is registered before sharing personal documents.
- 🚩 Research visa processes independently to confirm that what you're being told matches official requirements.

PrimePath UAE never charges candidates for job placement. If you're unsure about an offer you've received, contact us to verify.
